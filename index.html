About Mehwar Travels

Lahore's Trusted Travel Curators

Mehwar Travels is Lahore's premier luxury travel consultancy, headquartered at Tele Tower, Model Town. We specialize in curated international travel experiences for couples, families, and experience-driven adventurers across Pakistan.

Our expert team crafts personalized journeys to over 50 destinations worldwide — from the romantic streets of Istanbul and the terraced fields of Cappadocia to the beaches of Bali, the grandeur of Paris, and the crystal waters of Mauritius and Nassau.

We handle every detail: visa facilitation, premium hotel bookings, group tours, honeymoon arrangements, birthday surprises, and bespoke family getaways. Our group tours depart from Lahore with full support from certified Pakistani tour guides and international travel leaders.

Whether you're searching for Turkey tours from Lahore, honeymoon packages to Turkey, luxury Bali vacations, or budget-conscious group travel to Azerbaijan — Mehwar Travels delivers an experience far beyond expectations.
